UPCOMING EVENTS
Indonesian Independence Day Celebration

The team of AYANA and RIMBA invite you to join us in celebrating the 69th anniversary of Indonesia’s Proclamation of Independence, with a full program of events and activities including flag hoisting and national anthem, traditional games at our cliff-edge lawn, an event at UNIQUE Rooftop Bar & Restaurant, and the Taste of Nusantara buffet at Padi restaurant which also features performances of five dances from different parts of the country.
RIMBA Rooftop Spa

Offering the same world-class signature treatments as the spa at AYANA, the RIMBA Rooftop Spa by Thermes Marins Bali provides a new location for guests to enjoy their wellbeing experience, with a ‘back to nature’ design that differentiates it from AYANA’s traditional Balinese architecture, and magnificent rooftop views across RIMBA’s gardens, multi-layered pools and out to the ocean. To celebrate its opening, RIMBA Rooftop Spa is offering a complimentary Aquatonic Seawater Jet Pool thalassotherapy experience (treatment time up to 2-hours, valued at Rp399,000++) for all bookings of its introductory packages.
UNIQUE Pool Party

Join us 27 July for the Sunday Funday Pool Party at UNIQUE Rooftop Bar & Restaurant, with 2-for-1 selected cocktails, complimentary canapes, resident DJs and the new all-day dining menu available, offering a variety of modern Asian cuisine to match the innovative cocktails.
Light Up The Sky

The Japanese Obon holiday honors ancestral spirits and is always a festive occasion. To help our Japanese guests honor their ancestors while they are on holiday in Bali, we will light up the sky over the Indian Ocean with a fireworks show. All guests are welcome to join this event and enjoy the fireworks!
